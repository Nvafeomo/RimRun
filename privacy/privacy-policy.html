8. Controls for Do-Not-Track features

Some browsers and devices offer a Do-Not-Track (DNT) setting. There is no uniform standard for DNT yet, so we do not currently respond to DNT signals. If standards change, we will update this notice.

California law requires us to state that we do not respond to DNT signals at this time because no industry or legal standard for honoring them exists yet.
