7. Community voting

In short: You can vote to verify or flag courts. Use this feature honestly — abuse of the voting system may result in account action.

Logged-in users can cast a Verify or Flag vote on any court. These votes contribute to a court's community status:

  • A Verify vote indicates you believe the court information is accurate and the location is legitimate.
  • A Flag vote indicates you believe the court information is inaccurate, the listing is spam, or there is a safety concern worth admin review.

You may retract your vote at any time by tapping your active vote again. You may not cast both votes on the same court simultaneously.

Coordinated abuse of the voting system — such as mass-flagging legitimate courts or organizing false verify campaigns — violates these Terms and may result in account suspension or termination.
