Support

Questions, account help, or feedback? We read every message. Email rimrun.support@gmail.com.

Account & sign-in

Trouble signing in with email, Google, or Apple? Include your username and the sign-in method you use. Password reset is available from the login screen in the app.

Location & courts

If the map looks empty, check that RimRun has location permission while you use the app. To report a wrong court or suggest an edit, use in-app flagging or email us with the court name.

Safety & moderation

Report users or messages in the app. To appeal a moderation action, use the in-app appeal flow or email support with your username and what happened.

Privacy & your data

Manage visibility and messaging settings under Profile in the app. Read our Privacy Policy for how we handle data, including how to delete your account.
