Test RimRun on Android

RimRun beta builds are distributed through Google Play internal testing. You need an invite link from us and a Google account on your phone.

Invite link — When your track is live, we’ll share a Play Console opt-in URL here and by email.

ANDROID_TEST_LINK_PLACEHOLDER

Before you start

  • Android phone or tablet (physical device recommended).
  • Google account signed in on the device (Play Store).
  • Email address added as a tester in our Play Console list (we’ll confirm when you sign up).

Install steps

  1. Open the invite link we sent (or the button above once the link is set) on your Android device.
  2. Tap Become a tester (or similar) on the Play testing page.
  3. Accept terms if prompted, then tap Download it on Google Play.
  4. Install RimRun from the Play Store like any other app.
  5. On first launch, allow location if you want the court map; allow photos only if you set a profile picture.

What to try

  • Sign up or sign in (email, Google).
  • Open the map and browse courts near you.
  • Subscribe to a court and send a message in court chat.
  • Report anything broken to rimrun.support@gmail.com with your Android version and a short description.

Troubleshooting

  • “App not available” — Make sure you’re signed into the same Google account we invited, and that you accepted the tester opt-in.
  • Blank map — Toggle location permission for RimRun in system Settings.
  • Google sign-in fails — Try again on a stable network; email us if it persists.
